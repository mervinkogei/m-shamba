TYPES OF FARM PRODUCTS

Welcome to M-shamba where you get to post and purchase any farm products that you may need. Farm products are crops and livestock raised on farms.There are four different types of agricultural products. These are:

  • FOODS
  • FIBRES
  • FUELS
  • RAW MATERIALS

FOODS

Food classes include cereals (grains), vegetables, fruits, oils, meat, milk, fungi and eggs.

FIBRES

Fibre is a synthetic substance used to manufacture other products.they are mostly used in agriculture to make paper,cloth or rope.
